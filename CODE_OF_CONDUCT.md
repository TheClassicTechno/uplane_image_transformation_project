## Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive environment. Be respectful, collaborate thoughtfully, and assume positive intent.

### Expectations
- Be kind and constructive.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Focus on the work and the user.

### Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive or dismissive behavior.

### Reporting
If something feels off, open an issue or contact the maintainers.
